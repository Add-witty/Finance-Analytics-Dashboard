Personal Finance Analytics - SQL Practice & Dashboard Questions
Schema Context:Star schema in PostgreSQL with fact_finance and dimensions: dim_date, dim_category, dim_cashflow, dim_income_type, dim_scenario.

Level 1 – Basic SQL & Exploration
1. How many records exist in fact_finance?
2. How many unique users are present?
3. What is the average monthly income?
4. What is the average monthly expense?
5. What are the minimum and maximum income values?
6. How many records belong to each category?
7. How many users belong to each income type?
8. Distribution of financial stress levels.
9. Count of positive vs negative cash flow records.
10. Monthly income trend over time.
